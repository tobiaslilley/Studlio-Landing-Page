Privacy Policy for STUDL.IO

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

STUDL.IO ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID and basic profile information (name, email)
  • Health Data: Health metrics, protocols, adherence logs, and custom health tracking data you create
  • User Content: Notes, custom protocols, and any other content you add to the App
  • Communication Data: Messages you send to our support team

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features used, and time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Health Data from Apple Health: Metrics you've authorized us to access (heart rate, steps, sleep, etc.)
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crashes, and usage patterns (anonymized)

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Apple Health: Health metrics you've granted us permission to access
  • Apple Sign-In: Basic profile information from your Apple account

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Track your biohacking protocols and health metrics
  • Personalize Experience: Customize the App based on your preferences and usage
  • Generate Insights: Create charts, graphs, and progress reports
  • Send Notifications: Remind you about protocols and provide updates
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Health Data - Special Protections

4.1 Health Information Sensitivity

  • Health data is treated with the highest level of protection
  • We never sell your health information
  • Health data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Access to health data is limited to essential personnel only

4.2 Apple Health Integration

  • We only access health data you explicitly authorize
  • You can revoke access at any time through your device settings
  • We only read data; we never write to your Apple Health database
  • Data is processed locally on your device when possible

4.3 Data Minimization

  • We only collect health data necessary for App functionality
  • You control what health metrics to track
  • You can delete your health data at any time

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

  • We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information
  • We never sell your health data to third parties

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate the App (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (with notice)
  • Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5.3 Anonymized Data

  • We may use anonymized, aggregated data for research and improvement
  • This data cannot be linked back to individual users

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure Infrastructure: Data stored on secure, compliant cloud platforms

6.2 Your Role in Security

  • Keep your device and Apple ID secure
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Log out of shared devices
  • Report any security concerns immediately

7. Data Retention

7.1 How Long We Keep Data

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Health Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Usage Data: Typically retained for 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years for customer service

7.2 Data Deletion

  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Deleted data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all data we have about you
  • Update Information: Correct or update your personal information
  • Delete Data: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Export Data: Download your data in a portable format

8.2 Health Data Rights

  • Revoke Access: Remove Apple Health permissions at any time
  • Selective Sharing: Choose which health metrics to share
  • Data Portability: Export your health data in standard formats

8.3 Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control push notifications and email communications
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional communications
  • Updates: Choose how you receive important App updates

9. Children's Privacy

  • STUDL.IO is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents can contact us to review, update, or delete their child's information

10. International Data Transfers

  • Your data may be processed in countries other than your own
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers
  • Data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws

11. Third-Party Services

11.1 Apple Services

  • Apple Health: Integration subject to Apple's privacy policies
  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication through Apple's secure system
  • iCloud: Data syncing through Apple's cloud services

11.2 Analytics and Support

  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash data to improve App stability
  • Analytics: Usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Customer Support: Communication tools for assistance

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • We will notify you of significant changes through the App or email
  • Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance
  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically

13. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you're in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: For health data and optional features
  • Contract Performance: To provide the App's core functionality
  • Legitimate Interest: For App improvement and analytics
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

14. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate for exercising your rights

15. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: studlio@proton.me
  • Website: https://studl.io

16. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 2025 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
